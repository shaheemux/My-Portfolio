ABOUT ME

My name is Shaheem Salie, I was born and raised in Mitchells Plain, Tafelsig.
I am 24 years old and I completed matric in 2018.

I worked a full year as a driver assistant. The following year I studied IT
Technical Support while working part-time as a driver. Three months after
completing my IT course, I applied for work in a call center. The following

in my profession. Eventually, I came across Life Choices Academy and immediately got
drawn to Coding.
